Real-time simulation of violent boiling in concentrated sulfuric acid dilution

Watch the video

This project simulated the violent boiling phenomenon in real time. We integrated particle-based fluid solvers into an interactive VR application about concentrated sulfuric acid dilution experiment. Introduced the anti-viscosity method to inject momentum into high-temperature particles and capture the chaotic dynamics in the boiling process with small computational overhead.
I was in charge of the rendering. The fluids are rendered using Screen Space Fluids Rendering. The fluid rendering is accelerated with Compute Shaders. Integrated anisotropic kernels for better surface reconstruction.

Publication

CGI 2021 "Best Interdisciplinary Paper Award": Real-time simulation of violent boiling in concentrated sulfuric acid dilution. Wentao Chen, Tian Sang, Yitian Ma, Qian Chen, Yuwei Xiao, Zhigeng Pan, and Xubo Yang. The Visual Computer 37.9 (2021): 2631-2642. https://doi.org/10.1007/s00371-021-02208-0

Real-Time Fluid Simulation with Atmospheric Pressure Using Weak Air Particles

This project presents a particle-based approach to simulate versatile liquid effects under atmospheric pressure in real time. We introduce weak air particles as a sparse sampling of air. The weak air particles can be used to efficiently track liquid surfaces under atmospheric pressure. The method can be used to simualte interesting phenomenon such as Torricelli's Law experiment and trapped air bubbles.
I took part in the developing the particle-based solvers/ simulation demo applications and took charge of the rendering.

Publication

Real-Time Fluid Simulation with Atmospheric Pressure Using Weak Air Particles. Tian Sang, Wentao Chen, Yitian Ma, Hui Wang, and Xubo Yang. Computer Graphics International Conference. Springer, Cham, 2021: 151-164.

Simulate the chemical reaction between solid and liquid material

Fluid-solid coupling. Different results for solid cubes with different densities.



Concentration diffusion. Color indicates the diffusion of the product of the reaction.
